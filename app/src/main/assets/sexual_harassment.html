Sexual Harassment Laws

Sexual harassment laws address unwanted sexual behavior in workplaces and other environments, ensuring victims can seek redress and protection.

The Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013

This act aims to provide a safe working environment for women by preventing and addressing sexual harassment at the workplace. It mandates the establishment of internal committees in organizations to handle complaints and ensures appropriate redressal mechanisms.
