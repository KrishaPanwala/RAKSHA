Domestic Violence Protection

Domestic violence laws are designed to protect individuals from abuse by partners or family members. They offer support through shelters and legal protection.

Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005

This act provides protection to women who are victims of domestic violence. It includes provisions for safe shelter, legal aid, and compensation for victims. The act aims to safeguard women from physical, emotional, and economic abuse within the household.

The Family Courts Act, 1984

This act establishes family courts to handle disputes related to marriage, divorce, custody of children, and maintenance. It aims to provide a speedy and effective resolution of family-related issues, including those arising from domestic violence.
